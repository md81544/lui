  - Exit SGR mode in ~Terminal (std::cout << "\033[?1000l\033[?1006l";)
  - Return Keypress::MouseButtonPressX or Keypress::MouseButtonReleaseX on a click sequence:
      \033[<Cb;Cx;CyM    ← button press
      \033[<Cb;Cx;Cym    ← button release (same but final char is 'm')
